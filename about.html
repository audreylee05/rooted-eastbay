Our Story

Built by the East Bay, for the East Bay.

We grew up here. We watched families around us struggle not because resources didn't exist — but because finding them was a second job nobody had time for. Government websites were confusing, outdated, or in the wrong language. Resources were scattered across dozens of sites.

In 2022, a 5-person team of college students set out to change that. Over a 12-week sprint, we interviewed 20+ East Bay residents, surveyed 100+ more, and built RootED — a single, honest, easy-to-use platform that puts everything in one place.

We didn't assume we knew what people needed. We went and asked them first.
