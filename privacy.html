Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026. RootED is designed to help East Bay residents find resources quickly while minimizing data collection.

What we collect

We do not ask for your name, Social Security number, immigration status, or home address to use the screener and directory pages. Your screener answers stay in your browser and are not saved by RootED.

Analytics and cookies

We may use basic website analytics to understand page visits and improve content. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

External links

RootED links to third-party services such as county agencies and nonprofit programs. Once you leave this site, those services have their own privacy practices.

Information you submit

If you submit a resource suggestion, your email app opens with your details. The information you send is used only to verify and update listings.
